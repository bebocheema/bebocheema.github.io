Planet Omega || Tale of a True Love.

When you don’t know what to do in your life. It seems meaningless to you because the one thing which is your true desire has been driven away from you and you act like a pessimist and don’t do anything. You don’t know what to do now no one hears my words or can share a conversation with me, no one can care about you. There is a feeling of terror everywhere and darkness that covers the whole planet and there’s no light that can remove the darkness from its roots. This planet is just waiting for the sun to shine on its surface enlighten everything and remove the darkness from its roots. But the hunt for that shining star is still open and the planet hasn’t found anyone who can help him to forget about the stress and miseries of life and enjoy the feeling of love and the never-ending pleasure. Like a cold breeze with fragrance in it the little ecstasy that feels so amazing that the writer also wants to trench his thirst by that thing. In that world, there is a lone traveler he didn’t know about the upcoming problems that he has to face. He is just wandering to find True love in this chaos of people but everyone is greedy. But the traveler wandered around the planet just only to find his soul mate. He was wandering around the world for his true love but he had not yet found his true love. His story was not over yet, but that was the beginning of his story. He wanted a companion to take care of him and love him which he deserves to be. So, in the search for a soul mate, he started his journey and visited many parts of this planet that I am gonna share with you in this story.

So during this journey, he faces many ups and downs but he never loses his hope to search for his true love during this journey he meets many travelers who are going to a certain place and have different objectives to achieve but there is one thing in common in their objectives that is they all are running for money and wealth all they want is Wealth and they can do anything to achieve that but our traveler is very different from all of them he just wanted a true love who cares about him and have a feeling for him but he didn’t think anything about wealth, etc. He visited a Bazaar where one shop just caught his eye and go towards that shop to buy some delicious Sandwich and started eating them while he was eating he saw a young boy of age 9 years old trying to steal some bread from the bakery and while trying to steal that bread he gets caught and the by the Baker and he slaps the child he began to cry when traveler hears his cry his hearts melt and he saved the child from the Baker and brought the child the bread that he was trying to steal and the child. And asks him why is he stealing the bread so the child tells him his sad story which is

Boy: “I am an Orphan, My Parents died in a Road Accident and My Evil Aunt and Uncle threw me out of my own house and I became homeless but to survive in this harsh environment I have to steal because I have to eat something to live”

Traveler: “So, To Achieve the wealth that you are the owner of, you have to study hard.”

(The Traveler admitted the boy to the boarding school that is in a different city and Pay all the expenses of the school )

He Advises the Boy to Study Hard and he can visit him Occasionally (Then he started his journey again)
